about us

what makes our coffee special?

We make our coffee special by the way we make it, the ingredients we use, and through the rituals we associate it with. Coffee is much more to coffee lovers than a mere relaxing beverage or a quick energy drink. And we understand this.

The flavours in coffee come from the beans – and the flavour of these is influenced by where they are grown. Flavours are enhanced again during the roasting process, and then again by the brewing method of the final drink.
